About Us

Pet Friendly Hospitality

At Pacific Trails Resort, we warmly welcome your furry companions! Our pet-friendly yurts are designed to provide comfort for both you and your pets. Enjoy outdoor adventures with your four-legged friends, including scenic hiking trails and spacious play areas. We ensure a delightful experience for all guests.

Our Goal

At Pacific Trails Resort, our goal is to provide exceptional hospitality, blending luxury with nature. Our dedicated team of 50 staff members works tirelessly to ensure every guest enjoys eco-friendly accommodations and modern comforts. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to cleanliness, ensuring that every yurt, facility, and service is immaculate. Whether you’re seeking outdoor adventure or a peaceful retreat, we’re here to make your stay truly relaxing, memorable, and rejuvenating.
